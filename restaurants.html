Grab a bite

Our current best picks in town to get some grub!

SALVADORE'S

A rare gem away from the city center, but absolutely worth the short journey for a gorgeous Mediterranean-centric evenings meal in a friendly, family-run setting. Whether it's date night, or you're travelling with a large party, Salvadore's can provide a truly great evening.


KRISPY KEBAB

With Middle-Eastern culture strong in Germany, its glorious cuisine has gone from strength to strength. In no other dish is this as evident as the humble kebab. The Berlin style of Döner kebab is slowly taking over Europe, and with good reason! With fresh veggies and toppings, perfectly baked bread and the best Döner meat (delicious options available for vegetarian/vegan folk) you'll find in Bielefeld, you simply have to try this street-food style sensation. Thank us later!


NICHTSCHWIMMER

Brunch, lunch, dinner, cocktails, outdoor terrace in the sun. Need we go on? Be sure to stop by and check Nichtschwimmer out if you're in Bielefeld Center for some retail therapy. Kick back, relax, and enjoy some of the best food in town.
