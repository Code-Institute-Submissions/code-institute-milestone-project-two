ABOUT BIELEFELD

With more than 300,000 inhabitants Bielefeld is the largest city in the North Rhine-Westphalian Teutoburg Forest holiday region. Especially worthwhile is a trip to the Altstadt (“Old Town”) of this historical city, which is steeped in tradition and sees its first recorded mention in the 9th century. The historical old market square, with its ancient façades and mighty gables, is more than just a pretty face. It is also the cornerstone of event highlights such as the Bielefeld Nachtansichten (an “open night” at many cultural institutions), Leinewebermarkt (“Linen Weaver Market”), wine market and Christmas market. Also the Kunsthalle Bielefeld (art gallery) with its hall of modern and contemporary art exhibitions truly are a highlight for culture loving visitors of Bielefeld.
